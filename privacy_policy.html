PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated July 27, 2025

This Privacy Notice for twikia studios ("we," "us," or "our") describes our privacy practices for when you use our services ("Services"), including when you download and use our mobile application, TerraBlitz, or any other application of ours that links to this Privacy Notice.

Questions or concerns? Reading this Privacy Notice will help you understand our commitment to your privacy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services. If you still have any questions or concerns, please contact us at bytethebulletmobile@gmail.com.

1. WHAT DATA DO WE PROCESS?

Data We Do Not Directly Collect

We do not directly collect, store, or process any personal information from you that could be used to identify you, such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number. Our Services are designed to be used without the need for you to provide this information to us.

Data Processed by Third-Party Services

To provide core game functionality, our app utilizes third-party services from Google. While we do not receive your personal data from them, these services may process certain data to function.

  • Google Play Services (Play Games): If you choose to sign in to Google Play Games for features like leaderboards or achievements, Google will process your Google Play Games ID and game activity data (e.g., scores, achievements). This data is managed by Google.
  • Google Play Billing: If you make in-app purchases, all payment information is handled securely by Google. We do not see, receive, or store your credit card number or other sensitive payment details. We only receive a confirmation of the purchase to unlock content in the game. This constitutes "Purchase history" which is processed by Google.

We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle your data.

2. HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?

Since we do not directly collect your personal information, we do not use it for any purpose. Any data processed by third-party services is used solely to provide their respective features (e.g., displaying your score on a leaderboard, processing a purchase).

3. WILL YOUR INFORMATION BE SHARED WITH ANYONE?

We do not share any personal information because we do not collect it. Information related to your game activity (like leaderboard scores) is shared with Google Play Services to enable those features.

4. DATA SECURITY

We take reasonable measures to protect our application. However, data processed by third-party services like Google is secured by them. No electronic transmission or storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe we may have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us so we can take appropriate steps.

6. UPDATES TO THIS NOTICE

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Notice frequently.
